Our Full Body Painting Process

Meticulous steps for a perfect finish

1

Consultation & Color Selection

Discuss your vision, select your perfect color, and receive a detailed project plan and timeline.

2

Disassembly & Preparation

Careful removal of all detachable components and thorough surface preparation including stripping old paint if needed.

3

Priming & Sanding

Application of high-quality primer followed by detailed sanding to create the perfect base for paint.

4

Paint Application

Expert application of your chosen color in multiple thin coats in our controlled spray booth environment.

5

Clear Coat & Oven Curing

Premium clear coat application followed by oven baking for optimal hardness and durability.

6

Reassembly & Final Polish

Careful reinstallation of all components, final polishing, and detailed quality inspection before delivery.
