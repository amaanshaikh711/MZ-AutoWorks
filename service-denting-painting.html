Our Denting & Painting Process

Step-by-step professional approach for perfect results

1

Inspection & Assessment

Thorough examination of damage extent, surface preparation requirements, and color matching analysis.

2

Dent Removal & Body Work

Expert dent removal, panel beating, and surface leveling to restore original body contours.

3

Surface Preparation

Sanding, filling, and priming to create a smooth, paint-ready surface with proper adhesion.

4

Paint Application

Multiple coats of precisely color-matched paint applied in controlled environment conditions.

5

Clear Coat & Curing

UV-protective clear coat application followed by proper curing for maximum durability.

6

Final Polish & Delivery

Final polishing, quality inspection, and vehicle handover with your complete satisfaction.
