Scratch Removal & Polish

Restore your car's mirror-like shine with professional polishing and scratch repair

Bring Back Your Car's Showroom Shine

Over time, your car's paint accumulates scratches, swirl marks, water spots, and oxidation that dull its appearance. Our professional scratch removal and polishing services restore that deep, glossy finish that makes your car look brand new.

Using advanced machine polishing techniques and premium compounds, our detailing experts carefully remove imperfections while preserving your paint's thickness. The result is a mirror-like finish that reflects light beautifully and protects your investment.

Our Polishing Services

Comprehensive paint correction and protection solutions

Scratch Removal

Professional removal of light to medium scratches using multi-stage polishing techniques that don't compromise paint thickness.

Swirl Mark Removal

Eliminate ugly swirl marks caused by improper washing techniques and restore a flawless, swirl-free finish.

Water Spot Removal

Remove stubborn water spots and mineral deposits that etch into the paint surface over time.

Paint Correction

Multi-stage paint correction process to remove defects and restore your paint to better-than-new condition.

Headlight Restoration

Wax Protection

Premium wax or sealant application to protect your freshly polished paint and extend the shine.

Our Polishing Process

Expert steps for a flawless finish

1

Paint Inspection

Detailed paint thickness measurement and defect analysis under specialized lighting to assess correction needs.

2

Thorough Wash & Decontamination

Two-bucket wash method followed by clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants from the paint surface.

3

Compound Stage

Heavy cutting compound application with dual-action polisher to remove deeper scratches and defects.

4

Polish Stage

Fine polishing compound to refine the surface and develop maximum gloss and clarity.

5

Finishing Stage

Final polish with ultra-fine compound to achieve mirror-like reflection and depth.

6

Protection Application

Premium wax or ceramic sealant application to protect your newly perfected finish.
