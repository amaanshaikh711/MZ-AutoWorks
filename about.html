About MZ Autoworks

Your trusted partner in car care and repair

Our Story

MZ Autoworks is a family-owned car repair garage serving the Mumbai community with dedication and expertise. What started as a small workshop has grown into one of the most trusted names in car denting, painting, and mechanical repairs in the area.

We believe in honest work, transparent pricing, and treating every vehicle as if it were our own. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of hundreds of car owners who return to us time and again.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence by combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques and equipment. Whether it's a minor scratch or major accident damage, we approach every job with the same level of care and professionalism.
